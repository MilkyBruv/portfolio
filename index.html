About Me

My name is Malachi (Kai as short) McEnroe, I am an aspiring programmer, music producer, and indie game developer. I have always noticed a gap between what applications and video games are accessible to those with lower-end devices and hardware, so my goal to be able to create something that everyone can enjoy; reguardless of the limits of their hardware. I love to create my own video games without engines, relying soley on my programming knowledge and graphics libraries. For example: Swing and AWT for Java, and SDL/SDL2 for C/C++. I do this becuase I enjoy solving problems and watching projects take shape. Going engineless really made me understand what happens behind the scenes of games, and has greatly developed my programming abilities. Understanding that everything behind what is displayed on the screen is mearly maths was amazing to learn. And applying the knowledge I gained is even more fun.
